where's the milk?

Spilt Milk Pages's goal is to share my love of books with other pre-teens and teens my age, but also to review and promote diverse books that represent minority voices. Because I, myself, am a marginalized voice and grew up with no characters to relate to, Book Review Blog aims to provide recommendations and amazingly diverse book options and be a space for comfort and fun! Let's come together and share our love for reading!

Spilt Milk Pages is a book review blog run by a teenage girl hoping to connect other teenagers with good, diverse books!
